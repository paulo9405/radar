The proposal is to help online and physical sellers make better decisions about products by analyzing:

- market trends
- competition level
- saturation
- price range
- demand signals
- sales opportunity
- potential score/rating

Target audience:

- Shopee sellers
- Mercado Livre sellers
- Amazon sellers
- physical store owners
- resellers
- dropshippers
- affiliates
- people who want to start selling online
- small entrepreneurs

Main pain point:

Many sellers buy products without knowing if they will actually sell, enter saturated markets, waste time researching manually, and end up making decisions based on guesswork.
